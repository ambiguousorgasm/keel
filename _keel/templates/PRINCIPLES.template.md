# <Project Name> — PRINCIPLES

> The founding, near-immutable commitments of this project. PRINCIPLES sit above
> CORE. A CORE statement, decision, or implementation that violates a principle
> is a defect to investigate, not a new fact.
>
> Every principle below is **falsifiable**: a future change can be checked
> against it and the answer is yes/no, not a matter of taste.
>
> **Amendment process.** A principle is not changed via an ordinary `DECISIONS`
> entry. To amend, supersede, or retire one:
>
> 1. Open a task packet of type `principle-amendment`.
> 2. State the principle, the proposed change, and what in the project's purpose
>    has shifted to justify it.
> 3. Require independent reviewer sign-off from a different model family than the
>    proposer.
> 4. On approval, mark the old `P-n` as `superseded-by P-m` (never delete) and
>    append the new entry. Log the amendment in `CHANGELOG.md` under its own
>    heading.
